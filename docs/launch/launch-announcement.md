# Launch Announcement Draft

**Subject:** Introducing the AWS Community Content Hub (Beta)

Hi community friends,

We're thrilled to open the beta of the **AWS Community Content Hub**—a single place to catalogue, search, and report on community-created AWS content.

## Why it matters
- Automates ingestion from blogs, YouTube, GitHub, and conference talks.
- Provides unified search with semantic + keyword relevance.
- Generates program-ready reports for Heroes, Builders, Ambassadors, and User Group Leaders.
- Delivers GDPR-compliant self-service exports and account deletion.

## What’s included in beta
- Personal dashboard with analytics and badge tracking.
- Advanced search with saved queries and CSV exports.
- Admin console for moderation and badge management.
- Built-in monitoring, alerting, and backup safeguards for operational excellence.

## Call to action
- Join the beta waitlist: [https://awscommunityhub.org/beta](https://awscommunityhub.org/beta)
- Provide feedback in Slack `#content-hub-beta` or via email `beta@awscommunityhub.org`.

We appreciate your partnership as we polish the platform ahead of GA. Thank you for continuing to build the AWS community together!

— The AWS Community Programs Team
