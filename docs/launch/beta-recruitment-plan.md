# Beta Recruitment Plan

## Goals
- Recruit 15 community contributors covering Builders, Heroes, Ambassadors, and independent bloggers.
- Collect qualitative feedback on ingestion accuracy, search quality, and GDPR flows.

## Timeline
| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| T-21 days | Publish beta call-to-action blog & social posts |
| T-18 days | Direct outreach to AWS Community Builders cohort |
| T-14 days | Close application form & confirm participants |
| T-7 days  | Host onboarding webinar (30 mins) |
| T        | Beta launch (feature flags enabled) |
| T+14 days| Beta retrospective & go/no-go decision |

## Channels
- Blog post on dev.to + community.aws
- Email to AWS program mailing lists (Community Builders, Heroes)
- Slack `#aws-community` and Discord `AWS Cloud` servers

## Application form (hosted in Typeform)
Fields collected:
- Name & preferred contact
- Community program affiliation
- Primary content channels
- Time zone & availability
- Consent to participate and NDA acknowledgement

## Incentives
- $50 AWS credits for valid feedback submissions
- Featured highlight on launch announcement

## Success metrics
- 12+ active beta users (log in twice per week) — cohort roster tracked in `docs/launch/beta-cohort.csv`
- ≥25 feedback submissions
- Positive validation of GDPR workflows and analytics exports
